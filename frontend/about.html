Our Story

How We Began

Huejay International Multi Flora LTD was founded in 1983 by the visionary Hubert Jayakody. From humble beginnings, we have flourished and now operate five farms in the hill country.

Empowering Individuals & Communities

We take immense pride in our talented team of over 400 employees, 90% of whom are women. We actively support their personal and professional growth through various initiatives and opportunities.

Committed to the holistic transformation of our community, we reinvest 100% of our profits into programmes that provide financial support, educational opportunities, infrastructure development, free meals, and transportation.

Sustainable Practices

Our commitment to the environment guides every aspect of our operations. We employ sustainable methods that conserve water, minimise chemical use, and reduce energy consumption.
